
^yYour stats below reflect your physical and mental gifts.  They can be boosted through the use of ^HTraining points^?, which are granted at first level, and are gained at a rate of one per level.  These training points are also needed to gain new spells and abilities which your class qualifies for, but does not get at first level.^N

^ZStrength:^.^? Physical strength and fighting prowess is reflected in strength.

^ZDexterity:^.^? Agility and the ability to dodge blows is reflected in Dexterity.

^ZConstitution:^.^? Health, stamina, and the ability to live through harder fights is reflected in Constitution.

^ZIntelligence:^.^? Memory and concentration, and the ability to gain greater proficiency in skills is reflected in Intelligence.

^ZWisdom:^.^? Intuition, wit, and the ability to gain more practice points per level is reflected in Wisdom.

^ZCharisma:^.^? Grace, charm, and the ability to get better prices from merchants is reflected in Charisma.
